TLI Builder

Plan your perfect build

TLI Builder is a free, open-source build planner for Torchlight: Infinite. Theory-craft talent trees, configure gear, and preview your full stat sheet — all before you spend a single point in-game.

Download Discord GitHub
Ko-fi Patreon
Features
🌳
Talent Trees
Browse and allocate nodes across every talent tree. Search nodes by effect and see exactly what each point does.
⚔️
Gear
Browse the full legendary catalog, craft affixes, and equip items into gear slots to see how your loadout shapes up.
✦
And More
TLI Builder covers the full depth of your character — from hero progression to conditional mechanics.
Hero Traits Hero Memories Pact Spirits Slates Conditionals
📊
Live Stat Sheet
See your complete stat breakdown update in real time as you build. Every talent, piece of gear, and modifier accounted for.
💾
Build Saving & Sharing
Save multiple named builds locally. Import and export builds as files to share with others or back up your work.
Free & Open Source
TLI Builder is completely free to use. The full source is available on GitHub — contributions welcome.
